Levitum is a software product company with a distributed team based in Bangalore, Hyderabad and Chennai in India. Our team includes several early members of SupportSoft, and has over 70 man years of industry experience building enterprise and consumer products.

Our mission is to build fun, engaging apps that make people's lives easier.

We bootstrapped by offering high end mobile apps consulting, building iOS and Android apps that are in use by several million users. Around the end of 2013, we stopped consulting and have been fully focused on building our own products.

JustShare is a new product being developed by Levitum. JustShare enables you to publish content easily across a number of social networks. Unlike other tools, we place no restrictions on the number of connected accounts or limits on the number of posts being published.

We are known for having built Musiguru, the definitive music learning companion for Indian music. Our innovative technology lets students learn music by watching videos, and then record their practice sessions and share them with a global teacher network for feedback. Musiguru has been featured in the Wall Street Journal, The Hindu, Economic Times, India Today, App Advice and the Sruti magazine.

We apply lean startup principles to how we build our products, and blog about our experiments.

We're closely involved in the startup and tech communities, and give back to the ecosystem that supports us. We've been regular speakers at PyCon, Droidcon and several other events. We love reviewing gadgets, hanging out on Slack, traveling, and exploring new places to eat.

We're growing, and are looking for bright, energetic iOS, Android, Python and Web developers who would love to be enterprenurs themselves. While most startups don't have time for coaching, this is something we care deeply about. You'll have the opportunity to get ongoing mentorship from a patient, seasoned team of professionals who have built a real business.
